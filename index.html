Toolbox.vet

Toolbox.vet is a multi-media resource library geared towards educating professionals on veterans issues.

I researched and developed long-form content publishing solutions and integrated podcast and media hosting platforms to create a user-friendly library for easy navigation and access to media.

Vets Helping Vets

Vets Helping Vets is a Swords to Plowshares-produced podcast exploring veterans issues through sharing lived-experience of those who have served.

I created the landing page and developed branding & marketing strategies using Spotify and Anchor to effectively reach and engage veteran audiences.

Maceo May Affordable Housing Applications

This project was created with accessibility in mind - providing an online housing application for technologically-disadvantaged people.

I used Jotforms to streamline our internal housing application and improved the efficiency for our data collection process.
